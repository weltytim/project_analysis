Project Analysis

Site Intent

The intent of the website redesign is to condense the 57 pages into as little as 20 pages. The decrease in pages will also help redesign the flow of the site and increase the usability for the end user thus increasing web traffic and bringing in more visitors to the site and to the park. Reduction of the amount of content into these pages; Home, Explore, Gardens, Just for Kids, Rentals, About us, Contact us. We will condense the information to best captivate the end users, increase the flow of web traffic, and convince the end user to take a trip out to the botanical gardens.
